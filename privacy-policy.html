Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 17, 2026

Timio ("the app") is developed by an independent developer. This policy explains how the app handles your data.

Data Storage

All data you create in Timio — your selected cities, base city preference, and settings — is stored locally on your device using UserDefaults. The app does not transmit your data to any external server.

What the App Accesses

Timio does not request any device permissions. The app:

  • Does not access your camera
  • Does not access your location
  • Does not access your contacts
  • Does not access your photo library
  • Does not access any files on your device

All timezone data is bundled within the app and computed locally on your device.

Data Collection

Timio does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Specifically:

  • No analytics or usage tracking
  • No crash reporting sent to external services
  • No advertising or ad tracking
  • No third-party SDKs that collect data
  • No account creation or login required
  • No network requests of any kind

Third-Party Services

Timio does not integrate with any third-party services that collect user data. The app makes no network connections.

Data Sharing

Your data is never shared with third parties. It remains entirely on your device under your control.

Data Deletion

You can remove any city from the app at any time. Uninstalling the app removes all associated data from your device.

Children's Privacy

Timio does not knowingly collect any information from children. The app does not collect information from any user.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the revised version will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
